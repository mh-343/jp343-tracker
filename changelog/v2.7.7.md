## Features

- **Daily goal overflow**: Progress bar visually continues past 100% with a color change and milestone marker at the goal boundary.
- **Median stats**: Daily, weekly, and monthly averages now show the median alongside the mean for a more accurate picture of typical activity.

## Fixes

- **Paused sessions lost after 5 minutes**: Paused video sessions no longer get discarded when the browser restarts the background process.
- **Hourly activity skewed by bulk imports**: Bulk-added entries no longer distort the hourly activity chart.

## Improvements

- **Target start deviation labels**: Deviations over 60 minutes now display in hours format (e.g., +2h 22m instead of +142).
