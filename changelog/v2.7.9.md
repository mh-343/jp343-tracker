## Features

- **Popup resize**: Drag the bottom edge to adjust popup height; double-click to reset.

## Improvements

- **Tracking accuracy**: Time no longer accumulates when a video tab stops responding.
- **Session labels**: Manual tracking sessions now show their activity type in the session list.
- **Popup scrolling**: Session list and popup content are now fully scrollable.
