## Fixes

- **Spotify background tracking**: Fixed a bug that stopped Spotify from tracking while its tab was in the background, so the music and podcasts you play in the background are counted again.
- **Spotify reliability**: Spotify tracking recovers on its own if it ever gets stuck after an ad or a missed start, instead of waiting for a tab reload.
- **Streak after opening dashboard**: Your streak no longer changes to a wrong number after you open the dashboard.

## Improvements

- **Japanese channel detection**: More reliable at recognizing Japanese content, so channels with a Japanese name but an English title, like many Comprehensible Input channels, are picked up more often when you track only Japanese.
- **Compact popup**: The active session card takes less space, so more of your recent session history is visible at a glance.
