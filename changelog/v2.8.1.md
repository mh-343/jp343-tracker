## Features

- **Stretch Goals**: Five levels beyond your daily goal (150% to 300%), shown as a swipeable bar on the dashboard with per-level colors.
- **Popup level indicator**: Current stretch level displayed above the micro goal bar with matching level color.
- **L5 overflow**: Visual marker and color split when exceeding 300% of your daily goal.

## Improvements

- **Dashboard toggle**: Enable or disable Stretch Goals from the dashboard settings tab.
- **Content script cleanup**: Removed redundant page-unload handlers across all platforms.
