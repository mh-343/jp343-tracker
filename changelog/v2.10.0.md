## New

- **JLPT difficulty levels on YouTube**: Every video now shows an estimated level from N5 to N1, on the video and on cards in your feed, so you can pick something that fits. It works on any video with Japanese subtitles, worked out right on your device, and you can keep it fully on-device if you prefer.
- **Rate the level**: Signed in and watching a channel you know? A quick prompt lets you say whether a video felt easier or harder than the estimate, and enough community votes sharpen the levels for everyone.
- **Mokuro reading progress**: Your reading now syncs each volume's current page and whether you finished it, so your Reading tab on jp343.com shows how far along you are.
