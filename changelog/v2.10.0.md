## New

- **Difficulty estimate for any video**: While watching, a small chip shows the estimated level in JLPT terms, for example "~N4-N3". For any video that has Japanese subtitles, the level is worked out on your device from those subtitles, so even videos we have never seen get an estimate. A tilde means it is an estimate; a level without a tilde comes straight from the creator's own title tag like 【N3】.
- **Sharper where we know more**: When we already have data for a video or channel, the estimate uses it. Creator title tags win, then our analyzed per-video bands, then the on-device estimate, kept inside the channel's usual range where we know it.
- **Difficulty badges in the feed**: Levels also appear on video cards on the home page, in search results and in the sidebar, drawn from creator tags and our data, so you can judge videos before opening them.
- **Show difficulty levels toggle**: On by default. Turning it off stops everything, including the small daily data file fetched from jp343.com.
- **Local estimate only**: An option to work levels out purely on your device from the subtitles and never fetch anything from jp343.com.
