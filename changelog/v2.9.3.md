## Fixes

- **Mokuro tracking after updates**: Reading time keeps tracking in open Mokuro tabs after the extension updates, without reloading the page.
- **Mokuro access recovery**: If your browser turns off access to reader.mokuro.app, the dashboard and settings now show a Re-allow button that restores tracking and keeps reading time that was not counted yet.
- **Double counting**: Manually tracking reader.mokuro.app is blocked while Mokuro import is on, so the same reading no longer counts twice.
