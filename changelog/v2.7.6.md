## Fixes

- **Netflix tracking stops after 2 min**: Sessions no longer silently stop during normal playback.
- **Netflix sessions not saved on tab close**: Closing a Netflix tab now correctly saves the session.
- **Service worker recovery loses time**: Tracked time is no longer lost when the browser restarts the background process.

## Improvements

- **Faster ad-end detection**: Tracking resumes within 2 seconds after a Netflix ad ends instead of up to 30.
