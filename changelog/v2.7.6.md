## Fixes

- **Netflix tracking stops after 2 min**: Added missing buffer-resume handler that all other platforms had. Netflix buffering events paused tracking with no way to resume until the next 30-second heartbeat, causing accumulated time to fall below the save threshold.
- **Netflix sessions not saved on tab close**: The series-info wait path introduced in v2.7.5 skipped a required variable, preventing the extension from sending a save signal when closing the tab.
- **Service worker recovery loses time**: When Chrome's service worker restarts mid-session, the gap is now properly compensated instead of silently dropped.

## Improvements

- **Faster ad-end detection**: Netflix ad-end is now detected within 2 seconds instead of up to 30 seconds, so tracking resumes almost immediately after ads finish.
