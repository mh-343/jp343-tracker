## Features

- **asbplayer tracking**: asbplayer is now tracked as its own platform.

## Fixes

- **Account connection**: The extension no longer disconnects the account on its own; time keeps syncing.

## Improvements

- **Session recovery**: A clear reconnect prompt appears when needed, and syncing resumes automatically after logging back in.
- **Automatic platform updates**: New platforms (Twitch, Nihongo no Jikan, asbplayer) enable themselves, no manual toggling.
- **Twitch thumbnails**: Twitch sessions use the channel avatar as their thumbnail.
- **Clearer background settings**: The background control has its own label, and the opacity slider is now "dimming" to match its effect.
