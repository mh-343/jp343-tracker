## Fixes

- **Account connection**: The extension no longer disconnects your account on its own when a sync session needs a fresh connection token. Your time keeps syncing.

## Improvements

- **Session recovery**: When your jp343.com session expires, the dashboard shows a clear reconnect prompt and syncing resumes automatically once you log back in.
