## Features

- **Mobile difficulty badge**: The difficulty badge and the vote buttons now show on m.youtube.com, sized for touch.

## Fixes

- **Mobile channel detection**: Channel names on m.youtube.com are picked up correctly again after the YouTube redesign.
- **Extension updates**: Original titles and the Japanese-only filter no longer stop working in open YouTube tabs after an update.

## Improvements

- **Comprehension question**: Rating a video now asks how much you understood, with four answers from Nothing to All instead of level guesses.
- **Per-video votes**: Your answer counts for the exact video you watched and is remembered for that video.
- **Compact vote bar**: After you answer, the buttons collapse to a small checkmark; tap it to change your answer.
- **Japanese-only tracking**: On by default now, so only content detected as Japanese is tracked on YouTube and Twitch; you can turn it off in the settings.
