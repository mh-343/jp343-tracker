## Features

- **Anki tracking**: Your study time, reviews and retention are read straight from the Anki desktop app through the free AnkiConnect add-on.
- **Dashboard card**: Reviews, retention, mature retention, day streak, new cards and the words you know, plus a daily review sparkline.
- **Account sync**: When you are logged in, your Anki stats sync to jp343.com and show up in a dedicated Anki tab.
- **My-Hub extras**: Milestones, an immersion vs Anki balance, a full year review heatmap and a words you know growth curve on jp343.com.
- **Deck control**: Pick which decks count (leave out another language) and reset your Anki data whenever you want.
