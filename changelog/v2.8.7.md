## Fixes

- **Japanese video detection**: Japanese videos are recognized correctly, including on Firefox for Android and on titles that use decorative characters like hearts, so the tracking prompt no longer appears for them.
- **Channel list sync**: Allow and block list changes are retried when the server does not confirm them, so your channel choices sync reliably.
