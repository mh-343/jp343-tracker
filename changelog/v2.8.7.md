## Features

- **Streak-at-risk reminder**: An optional notification that warns you when your streak is about to break, so you can log today and keep it. Turn it on in Settings.

## Fixes

- **Japanese video detection**: Japanese videos are recognized correctly, including on Firefox for Android and on titles that use decorative characters like hearts, so the tracking prompt no longer appears for them.
- **Track only Japanese**: A non-Japanese video is no longer tracked when YouTube auto-detects its spoken language incorrectly (for example a German video about Japan). A Japanese video with a non-Japanese title is now also recognized from a predominantly Japanese description.
- **Channel list sync**: Allow and block list changes are retried when the server does not confirm them, so your channel choices sync reliably.

## Improvements

- **Streak grace**: A single missed day no longer breaks your streak, matching how streaks work on the website.
