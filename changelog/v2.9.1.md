## Features

- **Mokuro reading time**: Opt-in tracking that reads your manga reading time from reader.mokuro.app and counts it as reading immersion, synced to your account when logged in.

## Fixes

- **Monthly total**: "This Month" no longer shows more time than your all-time Total Immersion.

## Improvements

- **Twitch ad detection**: Sharper detection of Twitch ad breaks, so ad time is less likely to be counted as immersion.
- **Update reminders**: The prompt to reload after an extension update is clearer and less intrusive.
