## Features

- **Twitch tracking**: Live streams now count toward your immersion, with Japanese streams detected automatically and ad breaks left out of your time.
- **Nihongo no Jikan**: Lessons on nihongo-jikan.com are now tracked as their own platform.
- **CI Japanese rebrand**: Tracking continues seamlessly on the new nijapanese.com address.

## Improvements

- **YouTube Japanese detection**: Videos with kanji-only titles on Japanese channels are now recognized as Japanese instead of prompting you to choose.
