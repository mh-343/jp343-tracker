## Features

- **Recently deleted**: Deleted a session by mistake? Removed sessions are now kept for 30 days and can be restored from the dashboard, or cleared for good.

## Fixes

- **Firefox for Android**: Time tracking and settings work again on Firefox for Android. A startup problem could stop the extension from recording time or saving changes.
- **Difficulty chip**: The difficulty rating chip stays on Japanese videos only now, and the Japanese-only filter no longer acts on outdated data.
